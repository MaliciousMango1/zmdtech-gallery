The menu

Biscuits, meat, three sides.

Breakfast served until eleven. Lunch runs a meat-and-three, cafeteria-style, every weekday. Closed Sunday.

Cafeteria line. You tell us the meat, you point to the sides, we hand you a ticket with a table number. Drink and bread come with the plate. The lunch plate is a meat-and-two for $7.73 or a meat-and-three with bread and drink included. Prices below reflect our standard plates.

  • Three Pancakes $11.50 Choice of country ham, tenderloin, chicken, steak, or bacon. Syrup and butter.
  • Biscuit & Sausage Gravy $ — Split biscuits, thick peppered gravy, pork sausage. Add two eggs on request.
  • Country Ham Biscuit $ — Hand-cut, salt-cured ham on a fresh biscuit. The to-go order of choice.
  • Bacon & Egg Biscuit $ — Two strips, a fried egg, American cheese if you want it.
  • Cheese Biscuit $ — Buttery biscuit with sharp cheddar baked into the top.
  • Pork Tenderloin Plate $ — Two eggs, grits or hashbrowns, biscuit or toast.
  • Steak & Eggs $ — Hand-cut sirloin, eggs to order, grits, biscuit.
