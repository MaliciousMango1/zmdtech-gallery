Bistro menu · served daily

Lunch, made the way we’d want it.

Paninis pressed to order, salads built that morning, take-home dinners for the drive to practice. A rotating board of specials and a full coffee bar. Menu shifts with the market; here’s the core.

Salads

Available by the bowl for dine-in or packed to-go. Add grilled chicken $4, salmon $7.

  • Harvest Cobb $14 Roasted sweet potato, apple, bacon, bleu cheese, hard egg, grilled chicken, maple vinaigrette.
  • Bistro Chopped $13 Romaine, salami, provolone, chickpea, cucumber, tomato, red onion, red wine vinaigrette.
  • Strawberry Pecan $13 Spring mix, strawberry, candied pecan, goat cheese, poppyseed dressing.
  • Caesar $11 Hearts of romaine, parmesan, house focaccia crouton, anchovy Caesar.
  • Chicken Salad Plate $13 Scoop of house chicken salad over greens with grapes, tomato, and a warm croissant.
  • Side Salad $6 Mixed greens, cucumber, tomato, shaved carrot, choice of dressing.

Sandwiches & Paninis

Pressed on house ciabatta or served on sourdough. Comes with pickle and choice of one side.

  • Turkey & Brie $13 Smoked turkey, brie, fig jam, arugula, pressed ciabatta.
  • Caprese Panini $12 Fresh mozzarella, heirloom tomato, basil, balsamic glaze.
  • Roast Beef & Cheddar $14 House-roasted top round, sharp cheddar, horseradish aioli, caramelized onion.
  • Chicken Salad Croissant $12 All-white-meat chicken salad, butter lettuce, flaky house croissant.
  • Cuban Panini $13 Slow-roasted pork, ham, Swiss, pickle, mustard, pressed ciabatta.
  • Veggie Wrap $11 Hummus, cucumber, tomato, avocado, sprouts, feta, spinach tortilla.

Entrées & Take-Home

Chef’s rotating entrée board, plus chilled take-home dinners for two or four. Heating instructions on the label.

  • Braised Short Rib $32 Creamy polenta, roasted market vegetables, red wine jus. Serves two.
  • Chicken Marsala $28 Pan-seared chicken, mushroom marsala, buttered egg noodles. Serves two.
  • Shrimp & Grits $16 Georgia shrimp, andouille, stone-ground grits, scallion. Counter service only.
  • Lasagna Bolognese $ — House Bolognese, béchamel, parmesan. Serves four. Preorder by 10 a.m.
  • Chicken Pot Pie $ — All-white-meat chicken, root vegetables, flaky top crust. Serves two.
  • Vegetarian Pasta Primavera $26 Seasonal vegetables, fresh pappardelle, lemon-butter, parmesan. Serves two.

Sides

Included with sandwiches, or order à la carte by the pint.

  • Pasta Salad $5 Orecchiette, pesto, sun-dried tomato, pine nut.
  • House Potato Chips $3 Fried to order, sea salt.
  • Fruit Cup $5 Seasonal fresh fruit.
  • Broccoli Slaw $5 Shaved broccoli, apple, cranberry, sunflower seed, poppyseed.

Desserts

Every pastry is made from scratch in our kitchen. Whole cakes and tarts available with one day’s notice.

  • Key Lime Tart $7 Graham crust, fresh lime curd, torched meringue.
  • Flourless Chocolate Torte $8 Dark chocolate, sea salt, raspberry coulis.
  • Tiramisu $8 Espresso-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone, cocoa.
  • Seasonal Fruit Galette $7 Flaky pastry, whatever’s ripe.
  • Butter Croissant $4 Laminated in-house. Pair with a latte.
  • Bistro Cookie Box $ — Six cookies, rotating flavors.

Drinks & Coffee Bar

Full espresso program with trained baristas. Specialty lattes change with the season.

  • Espresso / Macchiato $3.50 Doubles standard. Ask for a cortado.
  • Latte / Cappuccino $5 Whole, 2%, oat, or almond.
  • Salted Honey Nut Cold Brew $6 House favorite. Cold brew, honey, salted hazelnut cream.
  • Chagaccino $6 Espresso, chaga mushroom, cinnamon, oat milk.
  • Lotus Energy Drink $6 Plant-based energy, house flavor combos.
  • Tea / Hot Chocolate $4 Loose leaf, Harney & Sons. Hot cocoa made with steamed milk.

Menu is a guide — specials rotate daily and some items are seasonal. Gluten-free bread and wraps available on request; please note any allergies when ordering.
