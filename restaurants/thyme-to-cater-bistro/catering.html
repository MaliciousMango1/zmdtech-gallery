Full-service catering · since 2009

The kitchen behind the bistro, on your event.

Chef Keith Mosser trained in Florence and apprenticed under a Michelin-starred chef in Germany before coming home to Jackson County. For 17 years he’s been cooking weddings, rehearsal dinners, corporate lunches, and the kind of private parties people talk about for months. Menu, staffing, rentals, cleanup — we run the whole night.

Packages

Starting points, built to suit.

Every event is custom. These are the formats most clients start with; we adjust menu, staffing, and service to fit the venue and the guest count.

Plated Dinner

$ — / guest

Three-course plated service with a staffed team. Ideal for weddings, rehearsal dinners, and formal corporate dinners.

  • Choice of two entrées, plus a vegetarian option
  • First course, salad, and dessert courses
  • Chefs, servers, and a captain on site
  • China, glassware, and linens available

Buffet Spread

$ — / guest

Casual, generous buffet service with attendants at the line. A favorite for rehearsals, graduations, and private parties.

  • Two entrées, two sides, salad, and rolls
  • Line attendants to carve and replenish
  • Disposable or rental tableware
  • Dessert station add-on available

Stations & Cocktail Reception

$ — / guest

Grazing tables, carving stations, and passed hors d’oeuvres. Great for mingling receptions, openings, and donor events.

  • Passed hors d’oeuvres selection
  • Two or three chef-attended stations
  • Charcuterie and cheese display
  • Staffed bar service coordinated

Corporate Lunch Drop-Off

$ — / guest

Boxed lunches and pan-served meals delivered to your office. Minimum 10 guests; next-day notice preferred.

  • Individual boxed lunches or hot pans
  • Delivered, set up, and labeled
  • Gluten-free and vegetarian options
  • Full beverage service available
