The Menu

Sample Menus

Organized by event. Every menu we serve is adjusted for your guest list, venue, and season — start here, and we'll build the final menu together.

I.

Weddings

Wedding receptions from intimate backyard ceremonies to 300-guest ballrooms. Plated service, buffet, or hybrid stations — with bar, rentals, and staff coordinated as a single estimate.

The Heritage Buffet $32 / guest

Two meats, four sides, salad, yeast rolls, sweet tea and lemonade, and dessert display. Our most-requested wedding package.

  • Carved pork tenderloin with peach chutney
  • Parmesan-crusted chicken
  • Southern green beans with bacon
  • Sweet potato casserole
  • Macaroni & cheese
  • Creamed corn
  • Broccoli & rice casserole
  • Garden salad with two dressings
  • Homemade yeast rolls
  • Dessert display (3 selections)
Plated Three-Course $48 / guest

First course, choice of two entrees, dessert. Full service staff included. Minimum 75 guests.

  • Mixed field greens, candied pecans, goat cheese
  • Filet medallions with bordelaise
  • Herb-roasted chicken breast, pan jus
  • Gruyere potato gratin
  • Haricots verts, shallot butter
  • Yeast rolls
  • Individually plated dessert
  • Coffee & tea service
Heavy Hors d'Oeuvres Reception $28 / guest

Six passed and stationed selections, designed to feed a standing reception for two to three hours.

  • Beef tenderloin biscuits with horseradish cream
  • Pimento cheese gougeres
  • Shrimp cocktail shooters
  • Bacon-wrapped dates
  • Grazing board — regional cheeses, charcuterie, fruit
  • Sausage ball station
Beverage & bar service, staffing, chinaware rentals, and setup are quoted separately based on venue and headcount. Tastings available by appointment once a date is held.

Corporate

Boxed lunches, conference breaks, board dinners, and full-day programs. Delivered on time, set up cleanly, and broken down without a trace.

Boxed Lunch $14 / guest

Individually packed. Choice of three sandwich or salad selections, chips, whole fruit, cookie, bottled water.

  • Smoked turkey & havarti on ciabatta

  • Grilled vegetable wrap
  • Chef salad with grilled chicken
  • Vegetarian / gluten-free options on request
Hot Lunch Buffet $19 / guest

Delivered hot in chafers, set up on-site. Choice of one meat, two sides, salad, rolls, tea, and brownies.

  • Herb-roasted chicken
  • BBQ pulled pork
  • Beef tips over rice
  • Mac & cheese
  • Green beans
  • Coleslaw
  • Tossed salad
Continental Break $9 / guest

Morning or afternoon refresh. Coffee, juice, fresh fruit, pastries, and yogurt.

  • House-baked muffins
  • Sausage biscuits (add $2)
  • Yogurt parfaits
  • Fresh fruit tray
  • Regular & decaf coffee
  • Orange juice

  • Sweet tea & lemonade
Southern Supper Buffet $26 / guest

A traditional sit-down supper presented buffet-style. Two meats, four sides, salad, and dessert.

  • Creamed corn
  • Broccoli casserole
  • Cornbread & yeast rolls
  • Peach cobbler
