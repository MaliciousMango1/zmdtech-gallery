Our Story

Forty-Two Years At The Table

A Winder kitchen serving the Athens region since 1984 — built on faith, family recipes, and the steady work of feeding people well.

We started in 1984 with one kitchen, one van, and a conviction that Southern hospitality is a professional standard, not a soft one.

Four decades later, we're still in Winder, still family-run, and still committed to the idea that a catered event should taste like someone's grandmother made it — and arrive like a professional crew put it there.

The Master's Table is faith-based and family-operated. We cater across Barrow, Jackson, Clarke, and the greater Athens region, with occasional runs into metro Atlanta for longtime clients. Weddings, corporate programs, graduation parties, holiday dinners, rehearsal suppers, Sunday-morning brunches after church — we've set the table for all of it.

What we care about: generous portions, scratch cooking, staff who show up early and leave the venue better than they found it, and a final plate that tastes the way the Sunday dinners of our youth tasted.

The Kitchen

80 Kelly Drive

Our dedicated commercial catering kitchen on Kelly Drive is where every plate starts. Walk-in coolers, dual convection ovens, smoker, fleet of transport hot-boxes, and the staging space to prep two 200-guest events in parallel — which we do most Saturdays from April through November.

Licensed, insured, and inspected. ServSafe-certified management on every event.

Milestones

Forty-Two Years

The short version. We've left the long version on the wall in the office — ask for the tour.

  1. 1984

    A kitchen, a van, and a first wedding

    Opened in Winder with one commercial kitchen and a van. First booking: a 120-guest wedding in Statham.

  2. 1992

    Expanded to corporate

    Began catering weekday conferences and board dinners at Athens-area firms. Still a core part of what we do.

  3. 2003

    Moved to Kelly Drive

    Built out our current commercial kitchen — the space we still work from today.

  4. 2014

    Thirty-year anniversary

    Catered 214 events that year. Our busiest season on record at the time.

  5. 2024

    Forty years on

    Still family-operated. Still catering seven days a week from April through December.
