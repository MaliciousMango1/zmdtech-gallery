Sunday brunch

Late starts, long pours.

Our Sunday brunch runs 11 to 3. Steak and eggs, benedicts, house bloody marys, and a quiet room to read the paper in.

From 11 am to 3 pm every Sunday. Brunch classics plus a few steakhouse crossovers — hanger steak and eggs, crab benedict, burnt cream french toast.
