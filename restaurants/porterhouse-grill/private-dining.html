Private dining & events

Milestones, meetings, and the occasional Bulldogs win.

Rehearsal dinners, engagement parties, graduation celebrations, corporate dinners, and every kind of gathering in between. We've been hosting Athens' most meaningful meals for almost three decades — and we've gotten good at it.

What we host

From a table of eight to a full buy-out.

We have the full menu, a full bar, and the flexibility to build an evening around your guest list, budget, and occasion.

Rehearsal dinners

An intimate private room or a semi-private section. Custom menus for 12 to 40 guests. We handle the pacing so you can handle the toasts.

Graduation parties

UGA graduation season is our busiest stretch of the year. Book early — we fill up by February for May ceremonies.

Corporate dinners

Client entertainment, board dinners, team celebrations. Pre-set menus, private rooms, and discreet service.

Anniversaries & birthdays

The milestone dinner, done properly. Custom cakes, champagne service, and a reserved table in our best corner.

Welcome dinners

Full restaurant buy-outs

How it works

From inquiry to dessert course.

A simple path. Most events come together in a week or two of back-and-forth — longer for buy-outs or peak-season dates.

1 · Tell us about it

Send the date, approximate guest count, occasion, and any preferences. We'll check availability and suggest options.

2 · Menu & details

We'll send a proposal with menu options, pricing, and room configurations. Adjust until it feels right.

3 · Host your evening

Confirm the guest count a week out, sign off on the final menu, and let us take it from there.
