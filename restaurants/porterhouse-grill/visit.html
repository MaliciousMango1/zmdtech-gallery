Getting here

Parking and directions.

Downtown parking fills up on Friday and Saturday evenings. Plan accordingly — especially around UGA home games.

Street parking

Metered spots line Broad and Thomas Streets. Free after 5 pm weekdays and all day Sunday. Use the ParkAthens app or bring quarters.

College Avenue deck

Two blocks west on College Avenue. First hour free — covers an appetizer course if service is running smoothly.

Valet

Complimentary valet on Friday and Saturday evenings from 5 pm. Pull up out front — the attendant will take it from there.

Before you come in

Good to know.

Reservations strongly recommended

Friday and Saturday evenings book up days ahead. UGA football Saturdays and graduation weekends book out weeks in advance.

Dress code

Smart casual. No jacket required. Come dressed like the occasion — we'll meet you at whatever level.

Kids welcome

We're happy to host young guests at any age. Children's portions available on most entrées — just ask your server.

Dietary considerations

Gluten-free, vegetarian, and allergen modifications are routine for us. Please flag your preferences when you book, or tell your server on arrival.

We're open tonight

Reserve your table.

Resy handles our reservations. If something isn't showing online, give us a call — we may be able to fit you in.
