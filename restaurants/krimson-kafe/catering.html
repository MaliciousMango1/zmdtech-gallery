Catering

Feed the office. Feed the party.

We cater small parties, office lunches, showers, and family tables around Oconee County. Drop-off trays, bagged lunches, pound cake boxes — whatever you need us to bring.

How it works

Tell us when, how many, and what you'd like. We'll send back a quote with options and a pickup time. For bigger jobs, call ahead — we bake cakes and make chicken salad to the order, so 48 hours notice is ideal.

  • Bagged Lunches Sandwich, chips, cookie, bottle of water. Packed and labeled.
  • Sandwich Trays Pimento, chicken salad, turkey, Ruben halves arranged on a tray.
  • Salad Bowls Wyllie, Spring, or chicken salad over greens — serves ten.
  • Soup by the Gallon Today's soup (or yours on request) packed hot with bowls and crackers.
  • Pound Cake Boxes Mini loaves, mixed flavors, sliced and ready. Good for shower tables.
  • Family Tables A Sunday-sized spread — sandwich platter, salad, sides, cake. Feeds twelve.

Pickup from the cafe is free. Local drop-off available for orders over a small minimum — we'll quote the drive when you inquire.
