The Private Room

Rehearsal dinners, small weddings, corporate dinners, and occasional buyouts. A quiet room, a shorter menu, the same kitchen.

The room

A dining room meant for long conversations.

We host private events most evenings of the week. The room seats groups of a dozen to several dozen depending on the layout; full buyouts of the dining room and bar are available for larger parties and for events that need the whole house.

Menus are built in conversation with the kitchen and almost always draw from the seasonal dinner list — tapas to begin, a shared appetizer course, a choice of mains, a dessert or two. Vegetarian and gluten-free arrangements handled as a matter of course.

Peter Dale and Erin Wilson have been hosting events in this room since it opened in 2007. Bring us the occasion; we will plan the night.

  • Seated Groups from roughly 12 up through the full dining room. Confirm capacity.
  • Buyouts Full-restaurant buyouts for rehearsal dinners, corporate gatherings, and celebrations. Available Monday through Saturday.
  • Menus Custom prix fixe, drawn from the seasonal menu. Tasting-style available on request.
  • Wine Pairings from the cellar, by the bottle or the pour. Corkage by arrangement.
  • Deposits A food-and-beverage minimum applies and varies by night. A deposit holds the date.
  • Lead time Two to four weeks is ideal; we will try for a last-minute dinner when we can.
