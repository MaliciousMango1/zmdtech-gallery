About the company

Started in a neighborhood bistro. Forty years later, still cooking.

What began as a bistro on South Milledge in 1986 has grown into Athens' most-booked full-service catering company — from UGA alumni weekends to black-tie weddings in the Historic Georgian ballroom.

Our story

Ron, Andrée, and a card-game name.

Ron Schwartz and Andrée Kosak opened Trumps in 1986 as a neighborhood bistro. The name came from card games — to "trump" something is to surpass it, outdo it, do it one better. That's held up as a mission statement for four decades.

By the mid-90s, catering had outgrown the bistro. Today we cook full-time out of our South Milledge kitchen, with an adjoining ballroom for up to 225 guests and a long-running partnership at the Historic Georgian downtown for larger receptions.

We've catered weddings for Bulldog legends, corporate dinners in UGA board rooms, reunion brunches for families we first cooked for when their parents got married here. Athens is our whole market. We keep it that way on purpose.
