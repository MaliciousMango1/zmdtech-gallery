Quick answers

A few things we get asked a lot.

How far in advance should I book?
Weddings: 8–12 months for spring and fall Saturdays. Corporate and conferences: 4–8 weeks. Rehearsal dinners: as soon as the wedding is booked. Holidays: by September for December.
Do you have a minimum?
Minimums vary by format and season. Box lunches start at 15. Buffet dinners at 40. Plated dinners at 40. Cocktail receptions at 50. Ask at inquiry.
Can you travel outside Athens?
Yes, within reason. We regularly cater in surrounding counties and occasionally in Atlanta for returning clients. Travel is quoted line-item.
Do you handle bar service?
Yes. We're licensed for beer, wine, and liquor service. Packages are quoted separately and we handle ABC permits for off-premise events.
