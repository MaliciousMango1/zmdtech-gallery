Getting there

From downtown

We're a few blocks off Broad Street toward the old rail corridor. About a ten-minute walk from College Square. On-street parking is usually easy outside of Saturday brunch rush.

Patio & seating

Large covered patio, shaded by trees most of the year. Indoor dining room with counter service at the register. Good for laptops between the rushes; noisy (in the best way) at lunch.
