Catering

Let us cook for your people.

We've been feeding other folks' gatherings for years. Weddings, reunions, church dinners, repast, corporate lunches, graduations, birthdays. If you're the one making sure everyone eats, let us take that part.

How it works

Tell us the date, roughly how many people, and what kind of event it is. We'll come back with a menu and a price. Nothing's set in stone until you say so.

Most orders go out as hot trays at pickup or drop-off. For larger events, we can staff the serving line and bring chafing dishes, plates, utensils, and tea service. If you need linens or a full setup, we'll coordinate with someone local and fold it into the quote.

Priced per half & full pan

  • Pickup or drop-off
  • Good for potlucks, office lunches, small family gatherings

The particulars

  • Lead time Forty-eight hours for most orders. A week or more for weddings and events over 75 people.
  • Minimum order 15 people for hot trays. No minimum for pan orders placed ahead.
  • Service area Greene, Putnam, Morgan, Hancock, and Oconee counties. Farther travel available for an added fee.
  • Deposit 25% on booking, balance due the day of the event.
  • Dietary Most sides can be made vegetarian. Let us know at booking and we'll accommodate.
  • Equipment Chafing dishes, sterno, and serving utensils available on request. Plates and utensils included for full-service packages.
