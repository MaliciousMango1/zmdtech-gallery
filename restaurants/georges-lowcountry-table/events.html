Private dining & events

Rehearsal dinners, tailgates, and the occasions that deserve a room.

Our private dining room seats up to forty for a sit-down meal. The food comes out of the same kitchen as the main room, drawn from the same board; we just build the menu a little ahead of time so everyone eats together. Tell us what the night is for, and we'll cook toward that.

Rehearsal dinners

Twenty to forty guests. A three-course menu we build with you the week before — often a raw bar to start, a choice of two mains, the bread pudding to end. Toasts welcome; we hand you the mic.

Full room with a short walk from the main dining room. Room fee waived at minimums.

Capacity · up to 40

Private parties

Birthdays, retirement dinners, anniversaries, the kind of Saturday that needs its own room. We can do a tasting menu, a plated course, or a family-style spread built around the boil. Wine list walked through in advance.

Typical buyout window: Tuesday through Thursday evenings or Sunday brunch service.

Capacity · 20 – 40

Tailgates & catering

Full-service catering for gamedays, corporate lunches, tailgates on the lot, and off-premise events. Lowcountry boils in the tailgate pan, trays of shrimp and grits, jambalaya by the quart, desserts by the pan.

Two weeks' notice is kind to everyone; we can move faster when the calendar allows.
