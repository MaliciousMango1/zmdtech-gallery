The Room

We took over a quiet brick-walled room across from The Arch and gave it back to Southern cooking. Old wood floors, warm lights, the kind of bar you lean into. Upstairs stays open a little later.

The recipes are our grandmother's, mostly, reworked for a table in 2026. The chicken is brined overnight. The grits are smoked gouda. The greens come from the farmers we've known since we opened.

We named it after our grandfather's car dealership. It felt right.

Alan & Ryan Pope · Owners
