Parking

Free gravel lot out front. Room for thirty-something cars. Extra overflow along the side of the building. No meters, no validation, no tickets. Handicap-accessible spots near the door and a ramp into the dining room.

If the lot looks full, it moves fast — tables turn every fifteen or twenty minutes at peak lunch.

Good to know

Cash and card both welcome. We have outdoor seating on the front patio when the weather's right. Takeout plates are available by the pound — tell whoever's at the register and they'll box you up.

Got a big group? Call ahead — we can push tables together for parties of eight or more.
