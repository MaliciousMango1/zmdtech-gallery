Catering

We'll feed your whole crew.

Pan trays, drop-offs, and by-the-pound orders. Office lunches, church suppers, family gatherings, funerals, anything in between.

Everything on the line can be packed into aluminum half-pans or full-pans and ready for pickup. We don't do flashy setups — we do honest food that feeds a room, hot out of our kitchen and into yours.

Hot meats by the pan

Fried chicken, baked chicken, meatloaf, BBQ pork, hamburger steak, beef tips, fried fish. Pick one or pick four. Half-pans feed roughly 10, full-pans feed 20–25.

Vegetables & sides

Collards, green beans, butter beans, mac & cheese, mashed potatoes, candied yams, field peas, creamed corn. Mix-and-match by the pan.

Breads & desserts

Cornbread by the pan, yeast rolls by the dozen. Peach cobbler, banana pudding, pound cake — sized for the crowd you're feeding.

Drinks

Sweet tea and unsweet by the gallon. Lemonade when the weather asks for it. Cups, lids, and ice included on request.

Takeout by the pound

No event? No problem. Walk in, tell us what looks good, and we'll weigh it up for you. Perfect for a supper at home or a plate for someone laid up.

Delivery

We can arrange local delivery within Athens-Clarke County for most orders. Ask when you call.

Ready to order?

Please give us 48 hours' notice when you can — more for bigger crowds. Give us a call and we'll walk you through it.
