Three owners, forty years, one kitchen.

The place opened in 1977 as Hanley's Hickory Pit and took the name Plantation Buffet in 1982. Keith Gray has run it since 1998. Same steam line, same cast-iron cornbread pans, same neighborhood regulars on their lunch break. We'll feed you.
