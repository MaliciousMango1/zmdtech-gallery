The Menu

A full plate, every weekday.

The buffet rotates through the week. Some things are on every day — fried chicken, cornbread, sweet tea. Others are waiting for their day.

Listed here is the usual rotation. The kitchen is allowed to have a mind of its own — we sub in what's fresh and what looks best that morning.
