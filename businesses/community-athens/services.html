Learn with us

Sewing classes, for everyone.

We run in-person beginner-through-advanced sewing classes in small group settings. Four-to-six week courses focused on clothing construction, mending, and alterations. Adult learners only, but all experience levels welcome.

Seats are limited and classes fill up. Reach out early in each season to reserve a spot.

Clothing construction

Mending & repair

Sewing for the Dawgs

Jersey repair for the University of Georgia football team.

Now in our sixth season working with UGA Athletics. We repair game-worn jerseys, add SEC graduate patches and bowl patches, and make custom-fit modifications that help players perform on the field.

It's a small way to extend the lifespan of one of the most-used pieces of athletic apparel in college sports — and a point of pride every season.

"Community has been an incredible partner. Their attention to detail, craftsmanship, and pride in their work are unmatched. Simply put, Community is the best in town."

— Gage Whitten, Director of Football Equipment & Apparel, UGA
