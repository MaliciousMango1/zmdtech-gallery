Our approach

Three ideas we're built on.

Most of the textile industry hides the people who make the clothes. We chose the opposite — a visible studio, a short supply chain, and makers we know by name.

i.

Sustainability

Everything in our in-house line is redesigned from vintage or handmade from sustainable materials. Less waste, longer lives, better clothes.

ii.

Transparency

Our sewing studio is right here in the shop. You can watch the work happen, ask questions, and see exactly where your clothes come from.

iii.

Connection

We teach sewing classes so the next generation of makers has somewhere to learn. Mending, constructing, and keeping good things in circulation.

What we do

More than a shop.

Sewing services, classes, gift curation, and yes — even a little football. Here's what we offer beyond the storefront.

Alterations & custom design

Hemming, formal dress alterations, vintage reconstruction, custom womenswear. All handled in our in-house studio.

Sewing classes

Sewing for the Dawgs

We mend jerseys for the University of Georgia football team.

Now in our sixth season. We repair game-day jerseys, add SEC graduate patches, bowl patches, and custom-fit modifications that help players perform better on the field.

It's a small way to bring sustainability — and Community — to one of the biggest stages in college football.

"Community is the best in town. Attention to detail, craftsmanship, and pride in their work are unmatched."

— Gage Whitten, Director of Football Equipment & Apparel, University of Georgia
