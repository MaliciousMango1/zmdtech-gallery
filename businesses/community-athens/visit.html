Getting here

How to find us downtown.

Downtown Athens parking fills up fast on weekends. Here's what works.

Street parking

Metered spots on N Jackson and Clayton. Free on Sundays and after 5 pm weekdays. Use the ParkAthens app or bring quarters.

College Avenue deck

Two blocks south on College Ave. First hour is free — plenty of time to browse.

From UGA campus

Five-minute walk from the UGA Arch. Head north on College Ave, turn left on Clayton, then right on N Jackson.

Before you come in

A few good things to know.

Shopping

Walk in any day we're open. No appointment needed. We're happy to offer complimentary styling sessions if you want a hand pulling pieces together.

Alterations & custom

Bring the piece in person for a quote. If it's a custom design from scratch, we'll schedule a longer consultation.

Sewing classes

Small group classes fill quickly. Email us for the current schedule and registration.

Gift boxes

Pre-made boxes are ready to grab. For build-your-own or corporate orders of 20+, give us at least a week's lead time.
