Full Service Breakdown

Services & Pricing.

Five core services, hand-quoted. Every price below is a starting point — sedans in typical condition. Trucks, SUVs, heavy oxidation, and neglected interiors are quoted after a short photo review.

01 — Paint Protection

Ceramic Coating

From$900

The long-term play. We fully decontaminate the paint, polish out what will polish safely, and apply a professional-grade ceramic that bonds to the clear coat and protects it for years. The coating is the easy part — the prep is where a bad install goes wrong.

Hunt for Details is a certified installer. Most passenger vehicles take 1–2 days on-site depending on paint condition.

  • Full two-bucket foam wash & wheel detail
  • Iron fallout & tar decontamination
  • Clay bar & mechanical decontamination
  • Paint depth gauged before polishing
  • Single-stage machine polish
  • IPA panel wipe prior to application
  • Professional ceramic — 3 to 5 year protection
  • Coating care kit & written aftercare
02 — Finish Restoration

Paint Correction

From$600

Swirl marks, wash scratches, light etching, and general neglect removed with a machine polish. One-stage for paint that's mostly there, two-stage for paint that needs serious recovery. Paint is measured before and during so we never go too far.

Correction is usually paired with a sealant or ceramic so the finish stays where we got it.

  • Paint depth gauge measurements
  • Decontamination prior to polishing
  • One- or two-stage compound & polish
  • Waterspot & etching removal (surface)
  • Hologram / buffer-trail removal
  • Machine-applied polish, never rotary abuse
  • Sealant finish (coating upgrade available)
  • Before/after photos on request
03 — Inside & Out

Full Detail

From$250

The complete reset. Exterior foam wash, wheels, jambs, and glass; interior vacuumed, steamed, and conditioned. Most sedans run 3–4 hours. Trucks, SUVs, and anything that hauls dogs or kids gets quoted up after a photo.

  • Two-bucket foam hand wash
  • Wheels, tires, wheel wells
  • Door jamb & fuel-door wipe
  • Streak-free hand dry
  • Exterior glass clean
  • Full interior vacuum
  • Steam clean high-touch surfaces
  • Leather condition (if applicable)
04 — Cabins & Carpets

Interior Deep Clean

From$200

When the inside has a story. Hot-water extraction on carpet and seats, upholstery shampoo, headliner safe-clean, leather condition, and odor neutralization. We pull a lot of pet hair. We're not squeamish.

  • Hot-water carpet extraction
  • Upholstery shampoo
  • Leather & vinyl cleaning
  • Leather conditioning
  • Headliner spot-clean
  • Steam sanitize of all plastics
  • Pet hair removal
  • Odor neutralization treatment
05 — The Extras

Restoration & Add-Ons

From$ —

The one-offs. Cosmetic restoration on older vehicles, headlight restoration, touch-up work, engine bay detail, chrome & trim refresh. Each one gets a real quote after we see photos — we'd rather undersell than oversell.

  • Headlight restoration & seal
  • Engine bay detail
  • Waterspot & etching removal
  • Paint touch-up (small)
  • Trim restoration & refresh
  • Chrome polish
  • Resale-prep package
  • Pre-coating correction prep
