Ceramic coating

Lock in the finish. Three durability tiers.

All ceramic installs run on a corrected finish — you're locking in the best the paint can look, not sealing in defects. Every coating is applied in a controlled environment with appropriate cure time.

3yr
Entry protection
$1,575

Single-layer SiO2 ceramic coating on paint and clear coat. Hydrophobic, UV-stable, easier maintenance washes. A smart upgrade for a daily driver you plan to sell in a few years.

5yr
Most popular

Deeper gloss, higher surface hardness, and stronger chemical resistance. Includes single-stage paint correction before install. The sweet spot between cost and longevity.

Flagship

Multi-layer ceramic system over a two-stage corrected finish. Maximum gloss, maximum durability. For builds, keepers, and vehicles you're not letting go.

Coating pricing is set per the brand lineup. Pricing shown is for sedans and compact SUVs. Full-size SUVs, trucks, and exotics may be higher — we quote after a walk-around. Paint correction is included in each tier as the prep step.
