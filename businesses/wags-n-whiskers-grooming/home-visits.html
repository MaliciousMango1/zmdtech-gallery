Home Visits

Grooming at your door.

For pets who do better at home — and for people whose schedules make shop drop-offs hard. We bring the groom to your driveway, porch, or laundry room.

Why we do it

Home is the calmer place.

Most grooming anxiety isn't about the bath. It's about the car, the other dogs, the stranger scents, the waiting. Take those away and a lot of pets settle right down.

We groom one pet at a time, in a space they already know. Seniors get to rest between steps. Anxious dogs get to sniff between tools. Cats don't have to leave the house at all.

You don't need a special setup. A garage corner, a bathtub, a covered porch, or a driveway works fine. We bring the table, the dryer, the shears, the shampoo, and the patience.

How it works

Four steps, start to finish.

  1. 01

    Request a visit

    Fill out the form below or give us a call. Tell us about your pet — breed, age, coat, and anything we should know.

  2. 02

    We confirm a date

    Home visits run on a neighborhood rotation. We'll offer the next two or three dates that match your area.

  3. 03

    We arrive & set up

    Typically 15 minutes to set up in your driveway, garage, or a corner of the house. You'll know when we're starting.

  4. 04

    Clean pet, clean space

    One to two hours per pet for a full groom. We pack out every tool, hair, and towel. You pay when it's done.

What to expect

Straightforward, no surprises.

We'd rather over-explain than leave you guessing. Here's the honest version of how a home visit goes.

  • We groom one pet at a time

    No rush, no stacking appointments. The whole visit is yours.

  • Water and power, when possible

    We can use your spigot and an outdoor outlet. If that's not workable, we'll talk through a plan — we've worked in apartments, condos, and barns.

  • We're honest about matting

    If the coat is heavily matted, we'll show you and talk through options before we cut. We will not hurt your pet to save a style.

  • Pricing is a range, not a surprise

    You'll get a starting quote when you book and a final price when we finish. Travel outside central Athens adds a small fee — we'll tell you up front.

  • Payment is easy

    Card, Venmo, cash, or check. Tips are never expected but always passed straight to the groomer.
