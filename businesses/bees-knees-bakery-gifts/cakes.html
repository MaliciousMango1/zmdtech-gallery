Custom cakes & cookies

Your cake, your way.

Our cakes taste as fabulous as they look. Pick a flavor, a filling, an icing, and a size — or bring us a Pinterest board and we'll design something together. From simple buttercream to fully fondant-covered.

How it works

Three steps from idea to pickup.

Give us 5–7 days of lead time for cakes, 3–5 for cupcakes, and a little extra for heavily decorated pieces.

Reach out

Email a rough idea, your event date, and how many people you're feeding. Photos or inspiration links help a lot.

We design together

We'll quote a price, lock in flavors and icings, and confirm the design. Deposits hold your spot on the calendar.

Pick it up, fresh

Come get it on pickup day. Boxed, stable, and ready for its moment.

Build your cake

Flavors, fillings, icings.

Mix and match however you like. If you can dream it, we can probably bake it.
