Before you come in

A few good things to know.

Walk in any day we're open for what's in the case. For custom orders, give us a heads up first.

Custom cakes

Order 5–7 days ahead. Email us or call to start the design conversation.

Pre-order cupcakes

Standard and mini cupcakes (12+ per flavor) require 3–5 days lead time. Gourmet sized are available in-store daily.

Wedding consults

Book an in-store consultation by phone — we'll schedule a tasting and design session.

Decorated cookies

Custom iced butter cookies start at $44 / dozen. Order with as much lead time as your design details require.

Lunch

Walk in between 10 and 6. Sandwiches, chicken salad by the pound, and boxed combos ready to go.

Questions

Call (706) 353-3633 anytime during business hours — we're happy to talk through ideas or pricing.
