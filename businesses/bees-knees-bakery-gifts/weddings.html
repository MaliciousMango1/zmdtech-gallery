Wedding cakes

Let us make the cake of your dreams.

From a simple two-tier to a showstopping sculpted piece, every wedding cake at Bee's Knees is tasted, tuned, and built from scratch — just for your day.

In-store consultations

Let's meet, taste, and design.

Every wedding cake starts with a consultation at the bakery. You'll sit down with us, taste cake and icing samples, and talk through your vision — the venue, the dress, the vibe, your flavor obsessions.

By the end of the appointment, we'll have a design direction, a flavor plan, and a firm quote. Book early — wedding season fills up fast.

How it works

From first call to wedding day.

A simple path — with plenty of room for your ideas along the way.

Call or email

Share your wedding date, venue, guest count, and a rough style direction. We'll check availability and schedule your consultation.

In-store tasting

Taste cake and icing samples, flip through design inspiration, and talk tiers, toppers, and flowers. We'll sketch a direction together.

Design & deposit

We lock in the design and flavors, send over a quote, and hold your date with a deposit.

Final details

About a month out, we confirm delivery or pickup, finalize guest count, and sign off on the design.

Baked & ready

We bake, tier, and decorate your cake fresh the day or two before your wedding.

Pickup or delivery

We'll hand it off at the bakery or deliver carefully to your venue — whichever works best for your setup.

What we'll talk through

Every detail, start to finish.

Flavors & fillings

Pick a flavor per tier, or keep it classic with one across the whole cake. All our cake flavors, fillings, and icings are available for weddings.

Design & decoration

Buttercream, fondant, florals, hand-painted details, or a sculptural finish — we'll work with your florist and planner if that helps.

Servings & tiers

We'll map tiers to your guest count so everyone gets a slice. Dummy tiers are an option if you want more height than cake.

Anniversary top tier

Want to save the top tier for your first anniversary? We'll box it up and walk you through how to freeze it.

Companion desserts

Pair your cake with cupcakes, decorated cookies as favors, or a full dessert table. Mix and match whatever fits the reception.

Dietary considerations

Gluten-free and other accommodations available for specific tiers or small companion bakes — let's talk through what's possible.
