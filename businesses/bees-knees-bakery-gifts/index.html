How custom orders work

Tell us what you're dreaming up.

Every custom cake starts with a conversation. Call us, email us, or walk in and we'll help you land on flavors, fillings, and a design that matches your event.

Reach out

Email beeskneesbakeryandgifts@gmail.com or call (706) 353-3633 with a rough idea, your event date, and how many people you're feeding.

Pick flavors & design

We'll walk you through the flavor, filling, and icing lineup — and sketch out a design together.

Pick it up

Order 5–7 days ahead for cakes (or 3–5 days for cupcakes). Fresh and ready on pickup day.
