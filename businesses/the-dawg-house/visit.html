Before you come

Vaccination requirements.

All pets staying with us — boarding, daycare, or grooming — need to be up to date. We'd rather catch a gap early than turn anyone away at the door.

Required

Rabies

Current Rabies vaccination for all dogs and cats. 1-year or 3-year accepted.

Required

Bordetella

Also called kennel cough vaccine. Required for all dogs in the building.

Required

DHPP

Distemper, hepatitis, parainfluenza, parvovirus. Standard canine distemper combo.

Send records ahead: Email vaccination records to office@thedawghousepetresort.com a few days before your pet's first visit. That way we can confirm your reservation. We also accept records in person at drop-off.
