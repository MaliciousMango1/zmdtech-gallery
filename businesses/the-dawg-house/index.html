A day at The Dawg House
  1. 7:00 AM

    Morning wake-up

    Potty break in the outdoor yards, fresh air, and first tail wags of the day.

  2. 8:00 AM

    Breakfast in suites

    Individual meals served in private suites, water bowls refreshed, medications given.

  3. 9:00 AM

    Group play begins

    Dogs sorted by size and temperament into supervised indoor and outdoor play yards.

  4. 11:30 AM

    Quiet time

    A midday break in climate-controlled rest areas — even the zoomiest pups need a lie-down.

  5. 12:30 PM

    Lunch service

    By request for boarding guests — we'll portion and feed from your supplied food.

  6. 1:00 PM

    Afternoon play

    Back to the yards for a second round of group play and structured yard time.

  7. 3:30 PM

    One-on-one enrichment

    Brushing, cuddles, and individual attention from staff who know your pup by name.

  8. 5:00 PM

    Dinner & wind-down

    Evening meal, a slower pace, and a last round of soft play before lights dim.

  9. 7:00 PM

    Goodnight

    Final potty break, lights dim, and overnight staff on duty until morning.

Every day of their stay, this is the shape of it.
