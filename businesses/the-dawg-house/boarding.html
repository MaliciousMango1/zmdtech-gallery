What to bring

Pack light. We've got the rest.

We supply bedding, bowls, and treats. But a few things from home help your pet settle in, especially on longer stays.

Please bring

  • Your pet's regular food, pre-portioned per meal if possible
  • Any medications with clear instructions
  • Vaccination records (Rabies, Bordetella, DHPP) if not already on file
  • A favorite toy or blanket that smells like home
  • Emergency contact info and your vet's phone number

Please leave at home

  • Expensive beds or bowls — we've got cozy ones ready
  • Valuables or collars with tags you can't replace
  • Rawhides or treats that need close supervision
  • Anything your pet doesn't actually use at home
How to book

Four steps. Done.

First-time guests, start with a phone call so we can talk through temperament and answer any questions. After that, booking takes a minute.

Call or email

Reach out with your dates. Holiday weekends and UGA home games book up well in advance.

Send records

Email vaccination records to office@thedawghousepetresort.com or bring them at drop-off.

Drop off

Bring your pet's food, meds, and any comfort items. We'll handle check-in and the tour.

Pick up refreshed

Add a bath or a full groom to the end of the stay so your pet goes home fresh.
