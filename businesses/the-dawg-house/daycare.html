Dog & cat daycare

A day of play while you're at work.

A supervised social hub where dogs make friends and burn energy. Separated by size and temperament, with plenty of indoor and outdoor space for romping and relaxing. Come home to a tired, happy dog.

The daycare day

Structured play, not chaos.

We group dogs by size and temperament before anyone steps into a yard. Shy pups get calmer company. High-energy dogs get playmates who can keep up. And pets who'd rather lounge than sprint have a quiet corner to chill.

Staff rotate through the yards all day, and every dog gets structured breaks for water, potty, and rest.

  • Dogs grouped by size and temperament
  • Indoor and outdoor play yards
  • Staff-supervised play all day
  • One-on-one playtime available — ball, tug, frisbee
  • One-on-one downtime for pets who prefer cuddles

Before your first day

What we need from you.

A few basics to keep every pet in the building safe. Call ahead for your first visit so we can do a quick temperament check.

Required

Up-to-date vaccines

Rabies, Bordetella, and DHPP for all dogs. Email records ahead or bring them to drop-off.

Required

Friendly temperament

Group play dogs need to be comfortable with people and other dogs. Call us about temperament screening.

Recommended

Spayed or neutered

For dogs over 6 months joining group play. Call us if you have a question about your pup.
