Before the appointment

What to expect on groom day.

We keep things calm, unhurried, and pet-led. If your dog is anxious about grooming, tell us up front and we'll build in extra breaks.

Book ahead

Call (706) 355-9911 to schedule. Regulars often book three or four appointments at once to stay on a rhythm.

Drop off & pick up

Most grooms take 2–4 hours depending on size and coat. We'll call when your dog is ready — no sitting in a crate waiting.

Vaccinations

Rabies, Bordetella, and DHPP required for all grooming dogs. Email records to office@thedawghousepetresort.com or bring them at drop-off.
