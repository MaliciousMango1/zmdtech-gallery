What we do

Four services, one clinic.

Chiropractic, neuropathy care, weight loss support, and ongoing wellness — coordinated under one roof so your plan stays coherent.

Service 01
Chiropractic Care

For back pain, neck pain, and the days that follow an injury.

Most people arrive here after trying to push through the pain on their own. A stiff neck after a long week. Lower back trouble after yard work. A lingering ache from a car accident that just never fully left.

Our chiropractic work starts with a careful exam and a conversation about how you actually move through your day. From there we build a treatment plan that's tailored to what the exam shows — not a one-size template. Adjustments are one part of it; so is education, so you understand what's happening and what you can do between visits.

If chiropractic care isn't the right answer for what you're dealing with, we'll tell you that too.

Service 02
Neuropathy Care

When your hands or feet feel like they're not quite yours.

Numbness. Burning. Tingling that wakes you up. A slow loss of balance you didn't notice until someone pointed it out. Peripheral neuropathy shows up in different ways for different people — and for a long time, most patients are told there isn't much to do about it.

Our neuropathy program is built around the patients we see most often: people who've lived with these sensations for months or years, who want a plan that takes the problem seriously. We spend time understanding the history, then put together a care protocol that addresses the contributing factors we can influence.

The first visit is a conversation and an exam. If we're a fit for your case, we'll walk you through exactly what the program looks like before you commit to anything.

Service 03
Weight Loss Support

A structured program, not a one-week cleanse.

Almost everyone who walks in for weight loss has tried things that worked for a while and then didn't. The frustrating part usually isn't losing the weight — it's keeping it off once the month of motivation wears thin.

Our program is built to remove the guesswork. You'll have a clear plan, regular check-ins, and a clinician to troubleshoot with when something isn't working. We look at what's happening in the body, not just what the scale says, and adjust as we go.

It works best for patients who want support, accountability, and a plan that actually fits the life they live.

Service 04
Wellness Care

Ongoing care for the version of you that feels good.

Wellness work is for the people who don't want to wait until something breaks. Maybe you want to move better, sleep better, or keep up with a life that's asking a lot of you. Maybe you've just finished a course of treatment and want to hold the progress.

We offer wellness visits that blend chiropractic care, gut-health coaching, and lifestyle conversations. The cadence is yours — weekly, monthly, or check-ins when you need them. We keep notes, track what's working, and adjust over time.

Most of our long-term patients end up here. It's how small things stay small.
