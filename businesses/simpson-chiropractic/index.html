Meet your clinician

A familiar face in Athens for more than thirty years.

Dr. Tony Simpson has practiced chiropractic care in Athens since the early 1990s. His work centers on patients who have tried other things first — and who want a thoughtful, patient look at what's really going on.

The practice has grown to include neuropathy care, wellness coaching, and a structured weight loss program. The thread through all of it is the same: find the root cause, explain it plainly, and build a plan the patient can actually follow.

Every new patient gets a full conversation before anything else. No two bodies are the same, and the plan shouldn't be either.
