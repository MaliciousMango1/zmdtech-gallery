Getting here

How to find a spot downtown.

Downtown Athens parking fills up fast on weekends and during campus events. Here's what works.

Metered street

Free on Sundays and after 5 pm weekdays. Bring quarters or use the ParkAthens app. Two-hour max on Broad.

College Avenue deck

One block north of the shop. First hour is free — plenty of time for a cut.

Walking from campus

Five-minute walk from the UGA Arch. Head north on College, turn right on Broad — we're on the corner.

Before you come in

A few things worth knowing.

Walk-ins only

We don't take appointments or book online. Just walk in — weekday mornings are our quietest stretch. Afternoons and Saturdays run longer waits.

Cash or card

Both work. Tips in cash are appreciated. The shop is one chair-and-a-register operation — no apps, no memberships.

Kids welcome

First haircut, twelfth haircut, whatever. Our barbers have been cutting hair long enough that nobody's first chair will rattle them.

Call ahead for wait times

If you're tight on time, give us a ring at (706) 353-9248 and we'll tell you how the chairs look.
