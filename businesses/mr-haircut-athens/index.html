About

An Athens institution, quietly running since 1968.

Joe and Raymond Duncan opened the doors on Broad Street in 1968 as the Flat Top Barber Shop. Pam Mullins bought it in 1994 and kept it running the way it always had — no appointments, no gimmicks, just good haircuts.

Today the chairs are worked by an all-women team of barbers who've been part of downtown Athens for decades. Students, professors, townies, game-day dads — everybody ends up in a chair eventually.
